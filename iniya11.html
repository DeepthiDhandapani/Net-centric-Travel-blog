Soul Travel to Mumbai

Mumbai is the best city of India to live in. It beats all other metropolitan cities of India in terms of things-to-do and safety aspect.
Mumbai is a mad mix of cosmopolitan culture. The locals are super cool people to hang out with and the city is so charming that even its chaos seems musical. The months of October to February are the best months to explore Mumbai.

Top things to do in Mumbai

Here is a list of top ten things to do in Mumbai. It was hard eliminating the other activities as the competition was tough!

  • Gateway Of India – Take A Ferry Ride
  • Marine Drive – Take An Evening Walk
  • Churchgate Station – Ride The Mumbai Local Train
  • Vasai Fort – Hike To The Top
  • Elephanta Caves, Gharapuri – Explore These Marvels
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus – Marvel At The Architecture
  • Bandra-Worli Sea Link – Go For A Long Drive
  • Juhu Chowpatty – Indulge In Street Food
  • Bandstand – Watch The Sunset
  • Kayaking – Get Adventurous!
